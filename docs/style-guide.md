
# Nerdy Sessions Brand Style Guide  
*AI Disruption Focus – React/TS/Tailwind*

## 1. Brand Overview

- **Mission**: Empowering individuals to navigate and plan for the societal impacts of AI.  
- **Positioning**: A trusted resource at the intersection of AI awareness, societal impact analysis, and proactive personal planning.  
- **Target Audience**: Lifelong learners, professionals adapting to industry shifts, concerned citizens, students, educators, and anyone seeking to understand and prepare for an AI-influenced future.  
- **Brand Personality**: Insightful, forward-thinking, balanced, empowering, accessible, pragmatic, and fostering constructive dialogue.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Deep Blue** (#101828): Used for primary text and dark backgrounds
- **Electric Purple** (#8C5CFF): Primary brand color, used for accents, buttons, and highlights
- **White** (#FFFFFF): Used for text on dark backgrounds and as negative space

### Secondary Colors
- **Light Gray** (#F9FAFB): Used for subtle backgrounds and dividers
- **Medium Gray** (#D0D5DD): Used for inactive states and secondary elements
- **Light Purple** (#EDE9FE): Used for secondary accents and highlights

## Typography

### Primary Typeface
- **Font Family**: InterDisplay for headlines, Inter for body text
- **Font Weights**: 
  - Light (300)
  - Regular (400)
  - Medium (500)
  - Semibold (600)
  - Bold (700)

### Text Styles
- **Headings**: 
  - H1: 48px/56px, Bold
  - H2: 36px/44px, Bold
  - H3: 30px/38px, Semibold
  - H4: 24px/32px, Semibold
  - H5: 20px/28px, Medium
- **Body Text**: 
  - Large: 18px/28px, Regular
  - Regular: 16px/24px, Regular
  - Small: 14px/20px, Regular
- **Button Text**: 16px, Medium, uppercase for primary actions

## UI Elements

### Buttons
- **Primary Button**: Electric Purple background with white text
- **Secondary Button**: White or transparent background with Electric Purple border and text
- **Button Radius**: 8px rounded corners
- **Button Padding**: 12px vertical, 24px horizontal
- **Hover States**: Darken background color by 10% on hover

### Cards and Containers
- **Border Radius**: 12px for cards and containers
- **Shadows**: Subtle drop shadows (0px 4px 8px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.05))
- **Padding**: Consistent internal padding of 24px

### Iconography
- **Style**: Simple, geometric line icons with 2px stroke width
- **Color**: Match to surrounding text color or use Electric Purple for emphasis
- **Size**: 24px standard size, scaled proportionally when needed

## Photography and Imagery

### Style
- **Aesthetic**: Modern, clean, and tech-forward
- **Color Treatment**: Natural colors with subtle blue/purple tinting when appropriate
- **Subject Matter**: Technology, collaboration, innovation, and diverse users

### Graphics and Illustrations
- **Style**: Abstract geometric shapes with gradient fills, incorporating brand colors
- **Usage**: As background elements, section dividers, or to highlight key concepts
- **Animation**: Subtle, purposeful animations that enhance the user experience without distraction

## Voice and Tone

### Brand Voice
- **Characteristics**: Knowledgeable, innovative, accessible, and trustworthy
- **Technical Level**: Balances technical precision with clarity for non-experts
- **Personality**: Forward-thinking but approachable

### Tone Guidelines
- **Headlines**: Clear, confident, and benefit-focused
- **Product Descriptions**: Technically accurate but accessible, emphasizing practical applications
- **Call-to-Actions**: Direct, action-oriented, and value-driven

## Layout and Spacing

### Grid System
- **Base Unit**: 8px spacing grid
- **Columns**: 12-column responsive grid system
- **Margins**: 
  - Desktop: 80px side margins
  - Tablet: 40px side margins
  - Mobile: 24px side margins

### White Space
- **Principle**: Generous use of white space to create focus and improve readability
- **Section Spacing**: Minimum 80px vertical space between major sections
- **Element Spacing**: 16px-32px between related elements

## Accessibility Guidelines
- **Color Contrast**: Maintain WCAG AA standards for text legibility (4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text)
- **Text Size**: Minimum 14px for body text
- **Focus States**: Clearly visible focus indicators for keyboard navigation
- **Alt Text**: Descriptive alt text for all informational images

## Digital Applications

### Website
- **Navigation**: Clean, minimal top navigation with dropdown menus for product sections
- **Page Layouts**: Consistent header structures with clear visual hierarchy
- **Animations**: Subtle entrance animations for content as users scroll

### Mobile Optimization
- **Responsive Design**: Fluid layouts that adapt to all screen sizes
- **Touch Targets**: Minimum 44px × 44px for all interactive elements
- **Simplified Navigation**: Hamburger menu for smaller screens

### Social Media
- **Profile Images**: Use the symbol mark on solid background at appropriate dimensions for each platform
- **Cover Images**: Clean designs featuring product imagery or abstract brand patterns
- **Post Templates**: Consistent layouts with brand colors and typography for different content types
